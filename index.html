Hi, I'm Aryan - Founder & CEO at Fyndr. For me, building something isn't just about startups or tech… it's about taking a tiny spark of an idea and turning it into something people can genuinely feel and benefit from. Fyndr was born out of that mindset - to make everyday choices simpler and help people connect effortlessly with what they're looking for.

My journey into entrepreneurship began when I was just 14. What started as curiosity slowly turned into a passion for creating, experimenting, and understanding how ideas can shape real lives. Over the years, I've worked on projects that carried meaning, whether it was leading social initiatives, building platforms, or simply trying to solve problems that mattered to me. Every small win, every failure, every spontaneous idea taught me something about resilience and purpose.

I've poured my heart into ventures like Fyndr & Foodation India (NGO I created when I was in 11th grade) and other independent initiatives not because they were obligations, but because they allowed me to grow into someone who builds with intention and empathy. They helped me discover that impact isn't always loud - sometimes it's hidden in the quiet effort of trying to make someone's day a little easier.

At my core, I'm someone who loves creating, learning, and refining until things feel right. Entrepreneurship to me isn't a label - it's a journey of becoming bolder, kinder, and more thoughtful with every step. And with each new chapter, I hope to build things that make people feel understood, supported, and inspired.
