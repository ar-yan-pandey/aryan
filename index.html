Hi, I'm Aryan - Founder & CEO at Club Fyndr. For me, building something isn't just about startups or tech… it's about taking a tiny spark of an idea and turning it into something people can genuinely feel and benefit from.

What is Club Fyndr?
Club Fyndr is an AI personal assistant and AI productivity app that consolidates every inbox, calendar, and communication channel into one intelligent interface. Instead of switching between Gmail, Outlook, WhatsApp, phone calls, Slack, and calendar apps, Club Fyndr acts as your single unified AI inbox — scanning, prioritizing, and autonomously responding across all channels on your behalf.

Over the years, I've worked on projects that carried meaning, whether it was leading social initiatives, building platforms, or simply trying to solve problems that mattered to me. And with each new chapter, I hope to build things that make people feel understood, supported, and inspired.
